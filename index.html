Location: Edmonson County, Kentucky
Requirements: Vertical certification, personal gear
Group size: Minimum 4 persons

General Safety Requirements

Required Equipment

  • • Three sources of light
  • • Helmet, gloves, and boots
  • • Vertical cave equipment
  • • Personal gear (no sharing)

Required Training

  • • Vertical caving certification
  • • Caving first aid knowledge
  • • Cave rescue training
  • • Self-rescue capabilities

Conservation & Safety

  • • Clean gear required (White-nose Syndrome prevention)
  • • No drugs, alcohol, or intoxicating substances
  • • Pack out everything you pack in
  • • No digging or modifying cave passages
