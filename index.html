Southern Mammoth Project Area

Cave Access Waivers

Access to caves in the Southern Mammoth Project Area requires a signed waiver. Please select the cave you plan to visit below to view and download the required waiver.

Important: All participants must be 18 years or older and must sign a waiver before entering any cave.

Ferguson Entrance

Non-commercial vertical entrance to the Mammoth Cave System (FEMCS). Minimum 350 miles of complex cave passage. Vertical drop of over 80 feet.

  • Type: Vertical entrance
  • Minimum Group: 4 persons
  • Requirements: Vertical caving certification, personal gear
View Ferguson Waiver

Hatcher Pit Cave

Non-commercial vertical entrance cave located on private property. Wild cave with vertical pit entrance requiring technical vertical caving skills.

  • Type: Vertical pit entrance
  • Minimum Group: 4 persons
  • Requirements: Vertical caving certification, personal gear
View Hatcher Pit Waiver

General Safety Requirements

  • Minimum of 4 people per trip
  • Three sources of light, helmet, gloves, boots, and vertical equipment
  • Training in caving first aid, cave rescue, and self-rescue
  • Clean gear to prevent White-nose Syndrome transmission
  • No drugs, alcohol, or intoxicating substances
